I got my first camera at the age of 12. It was one of those pocket cameras of the mid 90s where the film container almost looked like opera glasses. They used these crazy flash cubes that once used displayed all the colors of the most weird chemical reaction. Can you still rembember that? If not, don't worry, you're probably just to young. I just wanted to tell you were my passion for fotography started. Later on I realized photography is not only about the process from reflected light to the image it's more about people and lives.
